NLP – Text classifier with Neural networks

Business case: Financial advisor enters a 5000-character free form text documenting their annual review with the client. It’s an audit requirement that the review text is documented extensively. 40-member team manually reads through the text rates them good or bad.

Solution: Supervised NLP classifier is built to classify the text and gives it a probability score of how good or bad the review text is. Historical review classification is taken as training data. Basic text preprocessing such as removing punctuations, numbers, custom stopwords, lemmatization is applied. Used combination of Word2vec and FastText for the vectorizer. Neural network with sigmoid at the final layer to give us a probability score of goodness of the review.

Tools Used: NLP Word2vec FastText vectorizer FiservOwnedCloud
TIME SERIES: GBS Transactions Forecast

Multi Input Neural Network: Semantic Data Type Detection

Business case: GIO(Global Infrastructure Operation) Team requires Semantic Data Type Detection model for FISERV owned internal cloud process. Correctly detecting the semantic type of data columns is crucial for data science tasks such as automated data cleaning, schema matching, and data discovery.

Solution: We introduce FIS-Semantic model, a multi-input deep neural network for detecting semantic types. We train FIS-Semantic on 686, 765 data columns retrieved from the VizNet corpus by matching 78 semantic types from DBpedia to column headers. We characterize each matched column with 1, 588 features describing the statistical properties, character distributions, word embeddings, and paragraph vectors of column values. FIS-Semantic achieves a support-weighted F1 score of 0.89, exceeding that of machine learning baselines,

Tools Used: Deep Learning, VizNet, DBPedia, Global Statistics, FiservOwnedCloud

Ridge Regression: Predict Account objectives with underlying holding data.

Business case: Every wealth Management Account is created with an objective which could be Income or Growth. Advisors work hard to manage account holdings so the account stick to the clients intended objective.

Solution: Built a Logistic regression model that will predict what is the objective of the account based on its holding % of various assets and strategies that the accounts is tied into.Supervised training data from the past is used.
